About Our Team

We are the New Space Team from New Mongol Kosen. For previous years, we have been competing in Cansat since 2022. This year, our Space Balloon team is made of fresh members, but we share one goal: to improve ourselves through every challenge. With passion and teamwork, we are ready to grow and take on new missions.

Our achievements so far include:

  • 2022 CanSat Japan Competition - 3rd Place
  • 2023 CanSat Japan Competition - 2nd Place
